Featured Recipe : Jollof Rice

This recipe is very popular in West Africa. It is easy to make and taste amazing.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomatoes(fresh)
  • Onions
  • Seasoning Cubes

Instructions

Heat oil in a pot and fry Onions and Tomatoes.Add your seasoning and then stir in the rice. Let it cook for 30 minutes.


Price:N7000N5500


Volume: 5Kg


Co2 emission are minimal when cooking this dish.


Chef's Notes


Make sure to stir frequently to avoid burning.
